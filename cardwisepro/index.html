Description:

CardWise Pro helps you get the most from every purchase by organizing your wallet, surfacing the best card per merchant category, and tracking rewards across all your cards.

Whether you have one card or a dozen, CardWise Pro turns confusing reward tables into a simple, actionable guide so you never leave value on the table.

Features

  • Smart Wallet: Store and organize multiple cards in one place.
  • Rewards Optimizer: See category-by-category reward rates and instantly know which card maximizes cashback or points.
  • Quick Add: Add cards fast and edit names, banks, and types.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Tailored card suggestions based on your active wallet.
  • Simple Management: Edit, delete, and reorder rewards and card details with an intuitive UI.
